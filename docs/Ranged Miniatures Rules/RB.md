<h1 style="text-align: center;"><b>Ranged Attack Bonuses</b></h1>

Ranged units may shoot at opponent’s miniatures engaged in close combat with allied miniatures or base-to-base with their own miniatures (yes, it happens more than you’d think). However, each missed shot results in a hit on the “cover” miniature. These bonuses are stackable (ranged attacks does not get melee attack bonuses).

 
<h2 style="text-align: center;">Full Vissibility</h2><div style= "text-align: center;">If you can see the  entire base of the target, get 1d6!<div>

<h2 style="text-align: center;">Steady Aim</h2><div style= "text-align: center;">If your miniature hasn’t moved yet this turn, whatever is your turn or opponent’s turn, get 1d6!<div>
