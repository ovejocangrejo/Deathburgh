<h1 style="text-align: center;"><b>Crossfire</b></h1>

Ranged units may shoot at opponent’s miniatures engaged in close combat with allied miniatures or base-to-base with their own miniatures (yes, it happens more than you’d think). However, each missed shot results in a hit on the “cover” miniature.


Here’s how it works:

1) For the Ranged Attacker, each Aggressiveness (A) d6 roll or Defence (D)
d6 roll that meets or exceeds their Aggressiveness (A+) value (if in
Aggressive Mode) or Defence (D+) value (if in Defensive Mode) is a
Success. Anything less is a miss. The Attacker will leave any miss dice
that have been rolled on the table.
