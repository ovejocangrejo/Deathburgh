## Ranged
Miniatures with range weapons, as pistols, bows, crossbows or throwing rotten tomatoes, whatever, only can attack from a distance and can’t shoot when locked in close combat. The combat resolution is the same sequence that above but taking in care this:

<h2 style="text-align: center;"> In melee combat, Ranged units may only attempt to dodge incoming attacks</h2>

<h2 style="text-align: center;">Likewise, a Melee miniature can only dodge incoming ranged attacks </h2>
